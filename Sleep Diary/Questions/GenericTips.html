1. Keep Regular Hours - maintaining consistent sleeping hours will programme your body to sleep better
  2. Write away your worries - keep a notepad by your bed, so if you remember something important you can write it down and then clear your mind. Write a to-do list for the next day before you go to bed
  3. Exercise - moderate, regular exercise will relieve tension and ensure you are tired at bedtime. Avoid vigorous exercise too close to bedtime however.
